How to Use
1. 🔎 Scraper Mode

Extract detailed data from Amazon listings page.

  • Input: Paste ASINs/URLs or upload a CSV.
  • Region: Select Marketplace (e.g. Amazon.com) & Enter Zip Code.
  • Language: Choose English or Native.
  • Select: Choose attributes (Title, Price, Images, Videos, AOD, etc.).
  • Run: Click "Start Bulk Scrapping".
  • Output: Download Excel/CSV or push to Google/Microsoft Drive.
2. 🛡️ Auditor Mode

Compare live data against your "Golden Record" Catalogue.

  • Select: Choose Audit Points (Content, Image, etc.) to create a Dynamic Catalogue Template.
  • Setup: Create a Catalogue manually or Upload the filled Template.
  • Run: Click "Audit Catalogue".
  • Result: See Pass/Fail discrepancies and detailed insights.
